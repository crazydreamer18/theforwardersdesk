What I Wish I Had When I Started

Hi, I'm Prajit — I've spent the last 3 years working in shipping and logistics, across customer service, sales coordination, and export operations, with a focus on sea freight and documentation.

Early on, I spent a lot of time searching for simple, practical resources that actually made sense on the job. Most of what I found was either too academic or buried in long manuals nobody has time to read.

The Forwarder's Desk is what I wish I had when I started — clear, no-fluff tools and references built for people who are learning the industry or handling shipments without a big team behind them.

If you're a logistics student, a new coordinator, or a small exporter figuring things out — these resources are made for you.
